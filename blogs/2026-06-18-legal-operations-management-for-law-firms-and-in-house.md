This guide examines how legal operations evolved into a strategic business function and covers five operational pillars: financial management and spend analytics, outside counsel management, contract management, technology and AI systems, and strategic planning and governance. It notes most in-house teams make their first dedicated legal operations hire when legal headcount reaches 5-10 lawyers or annual spend crosses $5-10 million, citing Harvey's use by 142,000+ legal professionals across 1,500+ customers including over 60% of AmLaw 100 firms.
