This overview defines legal tech as the category of software, data, and AI systems legal teams use to deliver, manage, and improve legal work. It outlines six foundational categories: practice/case management, document management and automation, AI-powered legal research, contract review and due diligence, e-discovery and litigation support, and client service/billing, noting Harvey is used by more than 142,000 legal professionals across 60+ countries including over 60% of the AmLaw 100.
